Churd's CHR Charging Planner

Get a quick feel for charging time, range added, and what it should cost to top up before you head out. These defaults are based on a chill Bay Area EV vibe: about 2 miles per 1% battery and around 1.5% per hour on a slow charger. The cost estimate uses PG&E EV2-A style residential pricing for San Francisco so it stays simple and useful.
